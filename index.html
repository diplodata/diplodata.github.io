Vrijwillige Taak

This is a voluntary task you can do to with laptop or phone that will demonstrate the some of the basic project methodology. Please feel free to contribute.

  1. Click on “New Query” in header bar
  2. Think of something to query open data trends - a word, words, or a “particular phrase” - that relates to the theme we discuss in the briefing
  3. Click “VERZENDEN”
  4. (optional) Click “Nog een antwoord verzenden” and go back to step 2

To view the collection click on “Queries spreadsheet” in the header bar. Feel free to edit this to further curate the list.
